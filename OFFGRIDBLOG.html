unit 1.0: ABOUT ME:

I'm a 46 year old New Yorker, and have been interested in the offgrid lifestyle since I was an Art student in 1991. Living offgrid you face many challenges and a reoccuring theme is technology, and my own epiphany came to me while I was living in a Cretan cave, near Matala in the south of the Island of Crete. I lived For 6 months in that cave, but I had already lived for ( months on the legendary Island of Gavdos so cave life was easy! My journey to CodeNation literally began life as an idea in a cave on a Mediterranean island. My training has been frustratingly brilliant! Alittle too much too fast but just when you feel lost-you suddenly "get it" as you progress through the sausage factory...

So I've gone from this....living in a cave, learning how to start living in harmony with nature...collecting sea salt off the rocks, foraging, fishing....

...to this!!! The exciting transition to understand computer coding, learning how to tell a computer what to do, instead of simply using existing programs...
